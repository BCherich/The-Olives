Who We Are

Men Called to Gospel Work

The Olives is a dedicated group of Seventh-day Adventist men united by a singular passion: to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ through song and mission. We take our name from the Mount of Olives — the place where Jesus prayed, taught, and ascended — as a constant reminder of our calling.

From worship through our male vocal band to bold missionary ventures into unentered lands, we believe every man has a role to play in hastening Christ's return. We serve, we sing, and we go — together as brothers in Christ.

Music Ministry

Singing the Gospel

The Olives Men Ministers Choir is more than a singing group — it is a ministry. Every song, every harmony, every performance is offered as a sacrifice of praise and a tool for evangelism.

Rooted in the rich SDA tradition of sacred music, our choir ministers in churches, camp meetings, evangelistic series, and community outreach events, believing that music opens hearts to receive the Word of God.

Reaching the Unreached

Missions to Unentered Lands

There are still communities and peoples who have never once heard the gospel of Jesus Christ. Olives Ministries is committed to changing that — one unentered land at a time, through pioneer evangelism, community development, and media outreach.

🗺️
Pioneer Evangelism

Entering new territories with the gospel for the first time — planting seeds of faith in communities with no prior witness.

🏘️
Community Outreach

Meeting physical and spiritual needs together — health, education, and hope alongside the preaching of the Word.

📖
Literature & Media

Distributing gospel literature and digital resources to spread the three angels' messages far and wide.
